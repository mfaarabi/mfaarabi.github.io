About

Hello World!⌗

My name is Farabi, the author of farabi.sh. I’m a software craftsman, a PC/PS gamer, a tech enthusiast, and an eastern asian pop culture aficionado.

Throughout my time as a Computer Science student and as a Product Engineer, I learned that the best way to learn anything is by teaching. One of the purpose as to why I build this personal website is to share what I learned by teaching, to validate my understanding and also to be useful to others. My motto is “To live is to give”.

I would also include what I’m enthusiast about in this website. The point is to showcase my interest and to attract those with the same interest so we can share our thoughts and talk about it together!

So, there you have it. Enjoy your visit!
